Welcome to my tennis website

The main focus is to create a learning environment for beginners or players who just need a few tips.

What is this site?

This tennis site there is my take of all things tennis. To me and many others tennis is a way of life. I've met a lot of amazing people that I would not have had I not been around this sport.

Who makes a tennis site?

No one... really but me. My name is Lin and I'm from New York. I love to play tennis and everything about the sport. I played in college and went on to coach it at many levels. I've learned so much from tennis and I continue to learn when I step on the court.

why a tennis site?

Because I am a passionate fan of the sport
